Why hello there! I'm Lynxmic (but you can call me Lynx). You might also know me under my older alias "solarixtech" if we've got along for a long time.

I am yet another human being that uses the internet a lot, and I mean *a lot*. I've actually been on the internet since 2012, later starting my very first YT channel in 2013. My other interests/skills outside of content creation are in (some) video games, web development, graphic design, as well as have a sort of fascination for outside sights.

As a little child I kept accumulating more and more knowledge, both on the technology side of things (which happened to become huge passion of mine, long story), content creation, and... in life, of course.

Inspired by other niche tech YT channels at the time, I started in 2016 what is known today as Lynxmic, currently the channel is at over 500+ subscribers and growing, something I'd have never expected (just like other creators at their beginnings!)

This is my personal website which should serve both as a portfolio of sorts, and as a gateway to my internet presence in assembly. What you see here should serve as a representation of what I do etc. I run my own blog on this website as well, where you can learn even more about me! I don't post often there; at the end of the day, it depends on time, mood and ideas to come up with content of any sort (not exclusive to the blog!)

My pronouns are he/they (I accept being referred to by either he/him or they/them), and my time zone is GMT+2 CEST (Central European Summer Time) - oh, did I mention I'm coming from Europe?

At last, any questions or issues about the website, don't hesitate to open an issue on the GitHub repository or send feedback through email or my Discord Server.
